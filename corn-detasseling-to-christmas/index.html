Corn Detasseling to Christmas

From my “Master Plan” all the way to Christmas, this section of our relationship was a lot of fun (especially Frederick Meyer Gardens!)

July 16th - Frederick Meyer Gardens

Ok, you already know that this was my favorite date. It was already an amazing date when I pushed you into the boardwalk and gave you a kiss, but it got some much better in the car, and I still can't believe we got stuck! Thanks to your quick problem solving skills we got out, but I will remember this day for a long time

July 29th - Asylum Lake Part 2

Asylum Lake is the place to be! We have had two very successful dates here and this one was a lot of fun. We walked along the trail again, and we picked up a flower (that you look very pretty with) and took some pictures with it, I still have that flower in my room!

August 18th - Longboarding

We went to the Portage Bicentennial Park and long boarded. We played with Hei Hei for a bit too, and then we went out to eat at Sonic, their milkshakes are so good!

August 22nd - Hudsonville Fair

We went to the Hudsonville fair, and I got to meet your family! My favorite part has to be the kiss on top of the ferris wheel for sure

August 28th - Nature Walk

We enjoyed a walk in Al Sabo, and we sure had fun shooting this photo!

September 16th - Paw Paw Prairie Fen Preserve

We went on a walk in the Paw Paw Prairie Fen Preserve, and we talked about our Computer Club. I then laid my head on your lap as we sat down on a nice bench and talked about life

September 24th - Bronson 5K

We volunteered for the Bronson 5K, and we went to go get a smoothie afterwards. (Note to self: Don't go to the Checkers in downtown Kzoo, it's creepy)

October 28th - Birthday Exchange

We took a nice walk at Portman again, and exchanged birthday presents. I wrote ypu a heartwarming letter and got you a puzzle with your favorite picture of us on it. You got me a cool Wall-e lego set, and an even cooler licence plate sign with my name on it! I'll probably take that with me to my dorm room and hang it up there.
